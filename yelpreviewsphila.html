Restaurant Reviews and Demographics in Philadelphia

A WebGIS comparison of Yelp and Trip Advisor rated restaurants in select Philadlephia neighborhoods.




This map shows the highest rated restaurants in the city of Philadelphia with crowdsourced data from Yelp and Tripadvisor.
Clustering of highly rated restaurantsoccurs in Center City, notably around Market Street. Outside this area pockets exist in West and South Philadelphia. This map does not necessarily indicate the location of the best food in Philadelphia, rather it is a reflection on preferences of TripAdvisor and Yelp users.
Hover over the clusters for specific restaurant information.





This map show the top ten most highly rated restaurants in six different neighborhoods of Philadelphia.
Northern Liberties, Queen Village, Brewerytown, Point Breeze, Olney and Mill Creek are included in this map. In this map, census tract level data is shown for each neighborhood. Are there any correlations in restaurant placement and household income?
Hover over each census tract to display demographic data for each neighborhood. Click on points to see restauraunt information.
