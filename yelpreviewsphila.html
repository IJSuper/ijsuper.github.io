Restaurant Reviews and Demographics in Philadelphia

A WebGIS comparison of Yelp and Trip Advisor rated restaurants in select Philadlephia neighborhoods.



What might the concentration of highly rated restaurants in Philadelphia say?



This map shows the highest rated restaurants in the city of Philadelphia with crowdsourced data from Yelp and Tripadvisor.
Clustering of highly rated restaurants occurs in Center City, notably around Market Street. Outside this area pockets exist in West and South Philadelphia. This map does not necessarily indicate the location of the best food in Philadelphia, rather it is a reflection on preferences of TripAdvisor and Yelp users.
Hover over the clusters for specific restaurant information.
